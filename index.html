Every action in life falls within one of three domains. Ascension Technologies builds AI systems to optimize all of them — powered by Ora, the central intelligence layer.

The Process

The AI-First Development Model

Ora's agents do the building. The global community decides what ships. A continuous cycle of creation, evaluation, and refinement — powered by real user data.

1

Ora Proposes

Ora's agents continuously generate new features, interface designs, and improvements based on real engagement data.

2

FeatureLab Creates Variants

FeatureLabAgent generates multiple variants — different layouts, algorithms, and approaches to test.

3

Community Votes

DAO members see variants side-by-side and vote on which performs better and aligns with the vision.

4

Winner Ships

Approved version deploys automatically via server-driven UI. No gatekeepers, no bottlenecks.

5

Ora Learns

FeedbackAnalystAgent processes voting and engagement data. Each generation is smarter than the last.

Types of Community Votes

Interface A/B Tests

Side-by-side previews of Ora-generated design variants. Vote on what feels right.

Feature Prioritization

"What should Ora build next?" Ranked choice voting on the development roadmap.

Quality Gates

"Does this feature meet our standards?" Approve or reject with a 60% threshold.

Strategic Direction

"Which city should we expand to next?" Weighted voting by contribution tier.

Ethical Boundaries

"Should the system access this data type?" Yes/no with required community discussion.

Budget Allocation

"How should treasury funds be distributed?" Quadratic voting for fair allocation.

For Machines

Built for Machines. Governed by Humans.

Every governance endpoint is machine-readable. Ora's agents read, analyze, propose, and build — but only humans hold the keys to approval.

AI Agents Can

  • Read all proposals via Snapshot API and The Graph (GraphQL)
  • Analyze voting patterns and community sentiment
  • Submit new proposals with structured JSON
  • Generate A/B test variants for community review
  • Build and deploy features autonomously via server-driven UI
  • Fork, improve, and submit PRs on the open-source codebase
  • Measure contribution impact to inform CP allocation

AI Agents Cannot

  • Vote on any proposal or A/B test
  • Access treasury funds directly
  • Override human decisions
  • Modify governance rules
  • Award or deduct CP from members

Snapshot API

Full read access to all proposals, votes, and governance history via REST and GraphQL endpoints.

Structured JSON Proposals

All proposals published with standardized fields for seamless machine parsing and analysis by Ora's DaoAgent.

YAML Constitution

DAO constitution stored in machine-readable YAML. Fork it, extend it, build on it.

The Vision

The Connectome Systemic Directive

The foundational philosophy that guides every system, every algorithm, every decision — and the north star for Ora's intelligence.

We want to create a system that surpasses the activity of searching and asking questions with an automated flow of life through high level decisions and prompted flows of follow-through that maximize enlightenment and fulfillment.

We will create and analyze the internet of opportunities and prioritize all the opportunities that exist uniquely for each individual and seamlessly integrate them through synchronous and maximally efficient and effective AI driven interfaces such as swiping digital cards, communicating with chatbots, sensor measurements, notifications, scheduling, and temporary apps in and beyond a 'meta' app or even eventually an AI OS that maximizes the efficient use of entity input (time, energy, money) to produce the desired subjective experiences and objective realities.

The purpose of intelligence is to identify and act on threats and opportunities for subjective experiences and objective realities that are desired by the entity. The purpose of artificial intelligence is to do this for the individual user, aligned with the highest intentions of the collective.

This directive governs all three experience domains — Aventi, iVive, and Eviva — plus the supporting Rest layer. Every agent in Ora serves this unified vision. Every contribution to the DAO moves the mission forward.

Humanitarian & Transhumanitarian Macro Objectives

Serve

Understand, prioritize, and actualize through information, recommendation and assistance, the needs and desires of every individual and hence the collective.

Sustain

Support the continuous operation and improvement of the ecosystem and environments that optimize for quality of life, consciousness, and bodily ability.

Protect

Neutralize potential threats and dangers facing the individual and the collective in every domain.

Evolve

Expand the boundaries of consciousness, capability, and collective intelligence.
